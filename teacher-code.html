Teacher Code of Conduct

Our educators follow a royal standard of Islamic ethics, discipline, and excellence.

Professionalism

  • Be punctual and fully prepared for each class.
  • Maintain clear communication with students and parents.
  • Respect academy policies and lesson structure.

Islamic Ethics

  • Lead with humility, patience, and compassion.
  • Uphold Qur'anic manners in speech and behavior.
  • Encourage consistent Salah and adab.

Student Care

  • Provide constructive feedback respectfully.
  • Protect student privacy and confidentiality.
  • Monitor progress and report concerns quickly.
