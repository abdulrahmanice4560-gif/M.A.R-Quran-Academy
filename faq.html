Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to help families start their Quran journey with confidence.

Are classes online or on-site?

All classes are conducted online through secure video platforms such as Zoom or Google Meet.

Is a free trial available?

Yes. New students can request a free trial class to experience our teaching methodology.

Which languages are used for teaching?

English, Urdu, and basic Arabic are used, depending on student preference.

How are teachers vetted?

All teachers are qualified Hafiz/Qari, trained in Tajweed, and evaluated for professionalism.

Can adults enroll?

Yes. Our courses are designed for children, youth, and adults with flexible scheduling.
